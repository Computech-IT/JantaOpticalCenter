For over three decades, Janta Optical Center has been Sikkim's most trusted destination for precision eyewear — where craftsmanship meets clarity.

The Janta Optical Story

Founded in 2011 in the heart of Pakyong (New Market), Sikkim, Janta Optical Center began with a single mission: to make premium, precision-crafted eyewear accessible to everyone. What started as a small local shop has grown into one of the region's most respected optical destinations, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Sikkim and beyond.

Over the years, we have partnered with leading optical manufacturers to bring you an unmatched collection of prescription frames, sunglasses, and specialty lenses. Every product in our collection is handpicked for quality, durability, and design — because we believe your eyewear is more than a medical device; it is a statement of who you are.

Our team of trained opticians and frame stylists work tirelessly to ensure that every customer leaves with not just the right prescription, but the perfect fit. We combine decades of expertise with modern diagnostic technology to deliver eye care that is thorough, compassionate, and personalized.

Today, Janta Optical Center continues its legacy of trust and quality — now with a growing online presence, making it easier than ever to explore our collection from the comfort of your home and have premium eyewear delivered right to your doorstep, anywhere in India.
